## About Us

Our students are natural inquirers, curious about creation and the world around them, spending an incredible amount of time making sense of how everything fits together, and we guide our students' curiosities to help them become capable, responsible, and ultimately independent learners.

Our classrooms and curriculum encourage students to explore new ideas, gain critical skills, and cultivate a love of learning. Our curriculum is taught in a way that incorporates students with different backgrounds and learning styles.

Parents choose Coca School because we offer academic, social and personal success for every student. Through opportunities to learn from the best, experiences beyond the ordinary, and the encouragement to achieve more than what they thought possible, we help our students succeed anywhere through our unique educational offer. We do all this by investing in our people, our schools, and above all, our students.

We believe there is no limit to what your child can achieve. We encourage every student to reach for their dreams, try something new, and above all, to be ambitious. We support their curiosity, ask them to reach for their dreams and to step outside their comfort zones and try something new.

We engage students by providing instruction based on individual interests and learning styles, which meets the students’ academic, social, and emotional needs.

We welcome families as partners in reaching and teaching the whole child.


## Our Mission

To challenge and inspire young people to be ambitious for themselves, their communities, and their world.

What does this mean for your child?

We believe there is no limit to what our students can achieve. So we encourage them to be ambitious. We support their curiosity, ask them to reach for their dreams and to step outside their comfort zones and try something new. 

Being ambitious inspires our students. It urges them to stretch themselves that little bit further. To be relentlessly optimistic. To be the best that they can be. And beyond that to make a difference, to make things better, throughout our world.

Ambition can take them anywhere. It's transformational. It's what makes the extraordinary possible. But it's only the spark. It takes passion, determination and commitment to make it a reality. It takes creativity and innovation. It takes resilience and courage. It takes confidence.

That's why our outstanding teachers nurture these essential skills through personalised learning for each child. We build on each student's individual strengths and passions. We create challenge in every lesson and every activity so that they continuously grow and learn. We offer unique experiences to open new opportunities.

Our family of schools empower our students to achieve beyond what they may have thought possible, academically, socially and personally.
